Kendall's Top Movies

Because You've Been Asking ;)

"My Big Sister"

Set in an Igbo village, "My Big Sister" is a classic, in my determination. The film highlights the story of a differently-abled woman who unexpectedly finds love, despite her mother's attempts to sabotage it. The village scenery, unmatched talent and chemistry between actors Ihumoa Sonia Uche and Maurice Sam, and humor of Ebube Obi make it believable, enjoyable, and authentic to African film. (Found on YouTube)

"The Order of Things"

This is a light-hearted comedy with a real element of cultural expectations. The story focuses on two brothers who must follow the traditional "order of things" in all of life's stages, according to their mother, including who can get married first. The two brothers undergo an adventurous journey on both pleasing mummy and getting what they both want! (Found on Netflix)

"Us and Them"

Set in China, this movie tells the tear-jerking story of a couple whose challenges throughout the years end in an unexpected manner. We fall in love with the characters through their different relationship stages, watching them discover what is important to each of them as individuals and as a couple. Ultimately, being true to themselves takes them on different paths. (Found on Netflix)
